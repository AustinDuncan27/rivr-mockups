About Sunset Yoga Studio

For six years, Sunset Yoga Studio has been Austin's premier destination for mindful movement and inner transformation. Our modern, welcoming space provides the perfect sanctuary for practitioners of all levels to explore the ancient art of yoga.

We believe that yoga is for everyone. Whether you're taking your first breath in warrior pose or you're an advanced practitioner seeking deeper connection, our certified instructors with over 10 years of experience will guide you on your unique journey.

Our philosophy centers around creating a calm, supportive environment where students can build strength, flexibility, and mindfulness while connecting with a vibrant community of like-minded individuals.

Our Services

Hot Yoga

Experience the detoxifying power of heated yoga. Our hot yoga classes combine traditional poses with elevated temperature to deepen stretches, increase flexibility, and promote profound cleansing from within.

Vinyasa Flow

Flow through graceful sequences that synchronize breath with movement. Our vinyasa classes offer dynamic practice that builds strength, improves balance, and creates a moving meditation experience.

Meditation Classes

Cultivate inner peace and mental clarity through guided meditation sessions. Learn various techniques to quiet the mind, reduce stress, and develop a sustainable meditation practice.

Beginner Classes

New to yoga? Our beginner-friendly classes provide a supportive introduction to fundamental poses, breathing techniques, and yoga philosophy in a non-intimidating environment.

Advanced Practice

Challenge yourself with advanced asanas and deeper spiritual exploration. These classes are designed for experienced practitioners ready to expand their practice and explore complex poses.

Private Sessions & Memberships

Enjoy personalized instruction with private sessions tailored to your goals. Monthly memberships offer unlimited access to all classes and special member-only workshops and events.
