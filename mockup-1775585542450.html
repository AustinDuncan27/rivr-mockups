About Maverick

Welcome to Maverick Barbershop, where tradition meets modern style. For 8 years, we've been providing Denton, TX with the finest cuts, fades, and grooming services.

Our shop combines the classic barbershop atmosphere you remember with contemporary techniques and styles. We believe every man deserves to look and feel his best, whether you're looking for a timeless cut or the latest trend.

Walk-ins are welcome, but appointments are preferred to ensure you get the personalized attention you deserve.
