About Maverick

For 8 years, Maverick Barbershop has been Denton's premier destination for classic cuts and modern style. We combine the timeless tradition of old school barbering with contemporary techniques to deliver the perfect cut every time.

Our shop embodies the authentic barbershop experience – where craftsmanship meets conversation, and every client leaves looking sharp and feeling confident. Whether you're looking for a quick trim or a complete transformation, we've got you covered.

Walk-ins welcome • Appointments preferred

Our Services

Men's Cuts

Classic and contemporary men's haircuts tailored to your style. From business professional to edgy modern looks, we deliver precision cuts that enhance your best features.

Fades

Expert fade cuts including high, mid, and low fades. Perfectly blended transitions that showcase our technical skill and attention to detail.

Beard Trims

Professional beard trimming and shaping to complement your haircut. We'll help you maintain the perfect beard length and style for your face shape.

Straight Razor Shaves

Experience the luxury of a traditional straight razor shave. Hot towel treatment included for the ultimate in relaxation and precision grooming.

Kids Cuts

Patient, friendly service for our youngest clients. We make sure kids feel comfortable while getting a great cut that parents will love.

Classic Styles

Timeless cuts that never go out of style. Pompadours, side parts, and other vintage-inspired looks executed with modern precision.
