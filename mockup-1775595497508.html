Our Story

Started as a family dream in 1999, Lone Star BBQ has been the heartbeat of Dallas barbecue for over two decades. We've built our reputation one brisket at a time, smoking low and slow with the same techniques passed down through generations.

Our late-night hours make us the go-to spot when that barbecue craving hits after midnight. We've fed everyone from night shift workers to party-goers looking for the perfect end to their evening.

This isn't fancy dining – it's authentic Texas barbecue served the way it should be: hot, smoky, and unforgettable.
