Our Mission

The SENAD Group is dedicated to creating inclusive environments where individuals with autism, disabilities, and mental health needs can thrive. We believe every person deserves dignity, respect, and the opportunity to reach their full potential.

With years of experience and a compassionate approach, we provide comprehensive support that transforms lives and strengthens communities. Our integrated model ensures seamless care from education through adult living.

Building Brighter Futures

Through education, care, and community support

Our Comprehensive Services

Special Education Schools

Specialized educational programs designed to meet the unique learning needs of students with autism and developmental disabilities.

  • Individualized Education Programs (IEPs)
  • Autism Spectrum Disorder support
  • Behavioral intervention services
  • Life skills development
  • Transition planning
  • Family support and training

Adult Residential Homes

Safe, supportive living environments that promote independence while providing necessary care and supervision.

  • 24/7 professional care staff
  • Person-centered care plans
  • Independent living skill development
  • Community integration activities
  • Health and wellness programs
  • Recreational and social opportunities

Community Support Programs

Comprehensive community-based services that help individuals participate fully in their communities.

  • Day programs and activities
  • Vocational training and support
  • Mental health counseling
  • Crisis intervention services
  • Family respite care
  • Community advocacy
