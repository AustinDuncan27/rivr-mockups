Our Mystical Story

In the enchanted streets of Salem, Massachusetts, where magic whispers through every cobblestone, Zodiac Cakes was born from a passion for the celestial and the culinary. Our bakery brings together the ancient art of astrology with the timeless craft of baking.

Every pastry we create is a work of art inspired by the zodiac signs, star charts, and the mystical energy that Salem is renowned for. We embrace the witchy vibe of our historic city, crafting treats that speak to the souls of those who seek something beyond the ordinary.
